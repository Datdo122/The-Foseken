Before light had a name..
before darkness learned to take shape...
There was only Him
A being that needed no purpose...
only existence
And then... He fell.
Not a death - But a beginning,
And we came
Not in worship
...but in hunger.
We found His bleeding corpse...
...and saw not a god - ...but a gift
We drank.
And named it...Salvation
We drink His blood to find life.
We forge our empire upon decaying flesh...
...of a creator who breathes no more
But listen closely...
that heartbeat slows.
His gift runs dry.
And the Church, consumed by utter dread...
...begins the hunt anew.
Drink, Bearer of the Mark.
Partake and transcend...
or decay.
Arise...our newborn god.
